Who I am

Caleo Martinez
Owner & Craftsman · CM Finish Carpentry

I'm the owner and sole craftsman behind CM Finish Carpentry. I started this business because I believe finish work deserves real attention — not a rushed crew trying to hit a daily quota or move on to the next job.

When you hire CM, you're hiring the person who shows up, takes the measurements, makes the cuts, and does the install. I take on a limited number of projects at a time so that every job gets done the right way, on schedule, and without shortcuts.
