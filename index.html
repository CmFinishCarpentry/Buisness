Why work with CM Finish Carpentry?

You’re not just hiring a randon crew — you’re working directly with the person who measures, cuts, and installs every part of your project.

  • ✓ Direct communication: No bouncing between estimator, office, and crew. You talk to the installer from day one.
  • ✓ Attention to detail: I only book what I can realistically handle, so your project isn’t rushed to get to the next job. No corners cut
  • ✓ Respect for your home: Drop cloths, vacuum, and clean edges so you don’t feel like you had a construction zone in your house when the job is completed
  • ✓ Transparent pricing: Clear, itemized estimates so you know what you’re paying for and what’s included.

Typical projects: baseboard and trim upgrades, new interior doors, accent walls, and built-ins in living rooms, bedrooms, and hallways.

Jobs are scheduled so that I’m in your home for the minimum time necessary, while still doing the job right — no dragging projects out longer than they need to be.
