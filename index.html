Why choose CM

You're hiring the person doing the work

Not a crew, not a middleman. You talk to me, I show up, I do the work.

  • ✓ Direct communication — No bouncing between estimator, office, and crew. You talk to the installer from day one.
  • ✓ Attention to detail — I only book what I can realistically handle, so your project isn't rushed to get to the next job. No corners cut.
  • ✓ Respect for your home — Drop cloths, vacuum, and clean edges so you don't feel like a construction zone moved in when the job is done.
  • ✓ Transparent pricing — Clear, itemized estimates so you know exactly what you're paying for and what's included.
  • ✓ Realistic schedules — Jobs are booked so I'm in your home for the minimum time necessary, without dragging projects out longer than they need to be.

Typical projects: Trim upgrades, new interior doors, accent walls, and built-ins in living rooms, bedrooms, and hallways.

I also take on less common carpentry jobs — if it involves wood and precision, feel free to reach out about your unique project.
