Common questions

What to expect

💬
How quickly do you respond?
Same day — calls, texts, and emails all get a response by end of day. If it takes longer, I'm on a job site and will get back to you as soon as I'm done for the day.
📋
How does the estimate process work?
I'll visit the space, take measurements, and put together a clear written estimate with itemized pricing. No vague totals — you'll know exactly what you're paying for before any work starts.
📅
How far out are you booked?
It varies by season. Reach out and I'll give you an honest timeline. I don't overbook, so when I give you a start date I stick to it.
🧹
What about cleanup?
Drop cloths go down before work starts. At the end of each day I vacuum, collect scraps, and leave the space in good shape. You won't feel like a construction crew camped out in your home.
